Frequently asked questions

What counts as an interview?
An interview is a single AI-guided buyer conversation — whether it's a won deal or lost deal. Each completed survey counts as one interview toward your monthly limit.
What is Intelligence?
Intelligence is our AI-powered analysis layer. It automatically identifies patterns, themes, and trends across your completed interviews — surfacing insights like common loss reasons, feature gaps, competitive positioning, and buyer journey friction points.
Can I change plans at any time?
Yes. You can upgrade, downgrade, or add interview bundles at any time. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.
Do unused interviews roll over?
Monthly interview allocations reset each billing cycle. Purchased interview bundles remain available until used.
